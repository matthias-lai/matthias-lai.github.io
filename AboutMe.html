About Me

Hi, thanks for getting to know me!

I'm Matthias Lai, a Journalism and New Media and Digital Design student at Fordham Lincoln Center. I'm currently Head Opinions editor for The Observer, the student voice of Fordham Lincoln Center, where I periodically write articles as well. I've also written a bit for Amendo.com, where I spent a semester as an intern. I grew up in Detroit and when I'm not studying at Fordham I live with my family in upstate New York. I love NYC, and I hope to take every opportunity I can to get to know the city better and explore different neighborhoods and cultures that it has to offer!

My professor wants us to brag so here are some of my notable achievements:

  • Jan 2023 article in The Observer won Best in Student Newspapers Online Award
  • FCLC Dean's list 2021-22 school year
  • National Merit Scholarship
  • Selected as one of four editors from The Observer to represent Fordham University at the 2023 Associate Collegiate Press conference in San Francisco
